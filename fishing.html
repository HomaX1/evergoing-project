Conventional fishing

This is where it started it all. Since 1981, we have dedicated ourselves to sourcing quality products and forming valuable partnerships with manufacturers from all over the world. After almost 40 years later, our network of manufacturers and factories related to fishing number more than 100, mainly in Korea and China. With our extensive knowledge of manufacturing process, we have partnered with various U.S. companies to develop and deliver premium line-up of high-performance conventional rods and equipment. This is the core business of our original equipment manufacturing (OEM) division and we plan on continuing to pour our heart and soul into this business.

Ice fishing

Much like conventional fishing, Evergoing has a long history of working with various ice tackle companies from all over the country. We have been in the fore-front for sourcing various parts and products from overseas, spending countless hours in those countries looking for the finest of the materials at affordable prices. From clothing, augers, shelters, fishing combos to safety items and everything in between, we are an expert at finding and delivering the perfect equipment for your ice fishing brand or a line up.

About us - evergoing products group

The Evergoing Products Group began manufacturing inflatable boats and dinghies over 35 years ago. The value these boats propelled “EPG” into major private branding relationships with some of the largest marine companies in the world. The affordable and well-constructed boats quickly found favor with families seeking marine recreation and the chance to get on the water.

The all-inclusive, quickly inflated, rugged boat in a carry bag is easily stowed in the hold of larger craft, or in the trunk of the family car. The company’s success propelled them into a broader category of watercraft that now include, rigid hull inflatables, white water rafts, kayaks and pontoons – frequently built for industrial applications.

EPG continues to design and develop a wide variety of private label outdoor products, including camping, fishing, hunting and outdoor apparel for large private venders, including some of the largest big box and online retailers in the marketplace today.

With continued and projected growth EPG is becoming much more than a sporting goods and outdoor resource. The company’s growing staff continues their focus on creating affordable, family-oriented fun with every product developed.

Creating jobs

Since the advent of Internet commerce, EPG has been successful providing readily shipped, imported items to the E-Commerce marketplace. While we continue to evolve and grow within this chain of distribution, we understand a broader and responsible opportunity to the vested, brick and mortar retailers and the families they employ within their communities.

We are doing so by developing high margin, exclusively branded products and programs that meet their capital investments and fit their specific business models.

In an old school fashion we are partnering with talented regional sales professionals to engage in valued, relationship-based marketing processes, conveying the attributes and advantage of our brick and mortar retail products.

Ethical sourcing

Our manufacturing sources are located throughout the world and many of our partnerships have endured for years – as they too understand and have committed to the larger view of global responsibility.

The Evergoing Products Group endeavor to embrace and demonstrate a broader role and perspective for the global community presents a unique and evolving opportunity. For the strongly-brand and well identified global organizations, company size and assets grant privilege to engage and lead in all aspects of business responsibility.

Gratefully, we all have the opportunity to view the missions and core values of our world’s greatest professional organizations – beyond the goods and services they are known for.

The truly great firms lead occupational wellness of their companies, a focus and due diligence to the environment, and philanthropic endeavors within their sphere of influence.

Community

Relative to the scope of our business, the Evergoing Product Group is driven by a small group of seasoned, multi-cultural and dedicated employees.

Ownership invests every effort in the security, longevity of employment and well-being of each member of our staff.

Powerfully simple products for real people

We believe that everyone from experts to novices deserve great products. Products that are easy to-use yet powerful enough to help them get their work done. We’re dedicated to building those products and getting them into as many hands as possible.
